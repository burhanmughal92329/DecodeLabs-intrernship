📊 Business Performance Executive Overview Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to monitor key business performance metrics, analyze revenue trends, evaluate product performance, and compare sales across different regions. The dashboard enables decision-makers to track KPIs in real time and make data-driven business decisions.

2. Short Description / Purpose
The Business Performance Executive Overview Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of an organization's sales performance between January 2023 and June 2025. It helps business leaders monitor revenue, sales volume, average order value, regional performance, and product-wise contributions through dynamic and interactive visualizations.
This dashboard is designed for executives, sales managers, business analysts, and decision-makers who require quick insights into business performance and revenue growth.

4. Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
📊 Power BI Desktop – Used for creating interactive dashboards and visualizations.
🔄 Power Query – Data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing.
🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Created KPIs, calculated measures, and dynamic calculations.
🗂️ Data Modeling – Established relationships between tables for accurate filtering and reporting.
🌍 Map Visuals – Used to visualize regional sales distribution.

6. Features / Highlights
📌 Business Problem
Businesses generate thousands of sales transactions every month, making it difficult for executives to quickly understand overall performance, identify high-performing products, compare regional sales, and monitor monthly revenue trends.
Without an executive dashboard, answering questions such as
Which region generates the highest revenue?
Which products contribute the most to sales?
How is revenue changing month over month?
What is the average order value?
How many units have been sold?
requires manually analyzing raw datasets.

🎯 Goal of the Dashboard
The primary objective of this dashboard is to provide a centralized executive reporting solution that enables stakeholders to:
Monitor business performance in real time.
Track important KPIs.
Compare sales across different regions.
Analyze monthly revenue trends.
Evaluate product performance.
Support strategic business decisions through data-driven insights.
📊 Walkthrough of Key Visuals
📌 Executive KPI Cards

The dashboard displays three high-level KPIs:
Total Revenue: $4.38M
Total Units Sold: 16K
Average Order Value: $2.92K
These KPIs provide an instant overview of overall business performance.

📈 Monthly Revenue Trends
A combined Column & Line Chart visualizes monthly revenue performance throughout the year, making it easy to identify:

Growth trends
Seasonal fluctuations
High-performing months
Revenue declines
🌍 Revenue by Region (Map)

An interactive map displays revenue distribution across different regions, helping users:
Compare regional sales
Identify high-performing markets
Understand geographical business performance
🍩 Revenue Share by Product Category

A donut chart illustrates the percentage contribution of each product category, allowing users to quickly identify which products generate the largest share of total revenue.
Categories include:
Tablet
Laptop
Printer
Monitor
Chair

📊 Revenue by Product
A horizontal bar chart compares total revenue generated by each product category, making it easier to rank products based on sales performance.

🎛 Interactive Filters
Users can dynamically filter the dashboard by:
* Date Range
* Region
* Product Category

All visuals update instantly, enabling deeper analysis without modifying the underlying data.
